# Department Module

The `Department` module manages the academic departments and specializations within the school.

## Purpose

-   **Academic Structure:** Organizes students and teachers into their respective fields of study.
-   **Resource Allocation:** Helps in mapping internship placements to relevant departments.

## Key Features

-   **Department CRUD**: Manage departments and their metadata.
