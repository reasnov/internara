## üèóÔ∏è Architectural Invariants (ISO/IEC 12207)

### 1. The "Architecture Police" (Pest Arch)
- **Modular Isolation**: Implemented precision isolation rules in `tests/Arch.php`. Domain 
  modules are now strictly forbidden from direct concrete coupling.
- **Service Layer Mandate**: Every domain service MUST now extend `BaseService` or 
  `EloquentQuery` from the `Shared` module.
- **Thin Component Rule**: Livewire components are now verified as "Thin Orchestrators," 
  delegating 100% of data orchestration to the Service Layer.

### 2. Identity & Persistence
- **UUID v4 Invariant**: Verified that all domain models utilize UUIDs via the `HasUuid` 
  concern for global identity integrity.
- **Identity Anchors**: Formalized `User` and `Profile` models as "Shared Identity Anchors" 
  accessible across modules for authorization and attribution.
