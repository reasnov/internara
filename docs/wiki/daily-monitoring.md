# Operational Monitoring: Journals and Attendance

This is the active phase of the internship where the system captures daily evidence of learning.

---

## 1. Daily Activity Logging (Journals)
- **Student Action**: Log daily activities and claim competencies mastered.
- **Mentor Action**: Validate the log entry and provide feedback.
- **Instructor Action**: Monitor the quality of reflections and competency attainment.

## 2. Presence Tracking (Attendance)
- **Student Action**: Perform Check-in and Check-out via mobile.
- **Reporting**: Supervisors can view real-time attendance logs to identify absences.

## 3. Supervision & Guidance
- **Action**: Instructors record mentoring sessions and on-site visits to document student
  progress.
