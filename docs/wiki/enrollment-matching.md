# Enrollment and Placement Matching

This phase connects the students to their industrial placements and assigns their supervisory
team.

---

## 1. Student Registration
- **Process**: Students log into their dashboard and apply for an available internship program.
- **Verification**: Administrators or Staff review and approve these registrations.

## 2. Official Assignment (Matching)
- **Navigation**: `Internship` -> `Placements` -> `Assign Student`
- **Action**: Pair an approved student with a company placement slot.
- **The Team**: Assign one **Instructor** (Teacher) and one **Mentor** (Industry Supervisor) to the
  student.
