# Initial Configuration: Institutional Foundation

After the **Setup Wizard** is complete, you must ensure the institutional foundation is solid.
This data forms the basis for all official documents and reporting.

---

## 1. School Identity
- **Navigation**: `School Management`
- **Action**: Verify your institution's name, logo, and legal details.
- **Why?**: This information is used for branding certificates and formal reports.

## 2. Academic Departments
- **Navigation**: `Departments`
- **Action**: Register the departments (specializations) available at your school.
- **Why?**: Students and Instructors are grouped by department to ensure relevant supervision.

## 3. Active Time Scoping
- **Navigation**: `System Settings` -> `Academic Year`
- **Action**: Ensure the correct **Academic Year** and **Semester** are set as active.
- **Why?**: Internara automatically scopes daily activities (Journals, Attendance) to this period.
